Platform Capabilities

Everything you need to run TikTok ad campaigns — automatically

Built for eCommerce operators who need campaign velocity without manual overhead.

📣

Campaign Management

Create, launch, and pause TikTok campaigns programmatically via the Marketing API. Budget allocation and scheduling happen automatically based on performance signals.

🎬

Creative Management

Upload and rotate short-form video creatives through the TikTok Creative API. Automatically test multiple ad variations and retire underperformers.

📊

Reporting & Measurement

Pull granular ad performance data via the Reporting and Ad Measurement APIs. Real-time dashboards show ROAS, CPM, and conversion data per campaign.

🎯

Audience Management

Build and sync custom audience segments to TikTok. Lookalike audiences are refreshed automatically as new customer data arrives.

🗂️

Catalogue Management

Sync product catalogues directly to TikTok Shop and Dynamic Ads. Price and availability updates propagate within minutes of a product change.

🏢

Business Center

Manage multiple advertiser accounts under a single Business Center. Role-based access controls and consolidated billing across all campaigns.

How It Works

From product to live TikTok ad in minutes

A fully automated pipeline from product discovery through campaign launch and optimisation.

Connect Your Catalogue

Integrate your eCommerce store. We sync product data, images, and inventory in real time via the Catalogue Management API.

Generate Creatives

Short-form video ads are produced automatically from product assets and launched through the TikTok Creative Management API.

Launch Campaigns

Campaigns go live via the Campaign Management API with targeting, bidding, and budgets set automatically per product category.

Optimise & Report

Performance data flows back via the Reporting API. Underperforming ads pause automatically; budget shifts to top performers.

About

A focused eCommerce technology company

NovaDrop Elite is a Luxembourg-based eCommerce technology company specialising in TikTok advertising automation for direct-to-consumer brands.

We build and operate the full stack — from product catalogue integration through creative production, campaign management, and performance reporting — using TikTok's official Business API suite.

Our platform serves eCommerce operators across Europe and the United States who want the reach of TikTok's advertising platform without the manual overhead of day-to-day campaign management.
